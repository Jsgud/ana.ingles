Unit 10: Get in Shape | E. Grammar

  1. A: I'm glad that this car is too expensive for us.
    B: I agree that it’s quite expensive. Maybe we should look for a cheaper one.

  2. A: This is her boyfriend? I can’t believe it! She says that he is her cousin.
    B: I know. You should never believe what Lora says.

  3. A: I think that we went on a hike yesterday.
    B: Yeah. It was really beautiful.

Unit 11: Money Management | C. Conversation I

Match questions and answers:

  • 1. We haven’t talked for ages. — c. Yeah. We should have coffee sometime.
  • 2. Do you like my new purse? — a. I love it. It looks quite expensive.
  • 3. Aren’t your parents well-off? — b. They get by, but I don’t want to take money from them.
  • 4. Why don’t we go out dancing tomorrow night? — e. I’d love to, but I’m totally broke right now.
  • 5. I just charge everything on my credit card. — d. That’s cool, but how will you pay it all back?
